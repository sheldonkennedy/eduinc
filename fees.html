School Fees for 2017

Our fees are comprised of four components: An initial registration/administration fee, an annual deposit, a monthly tuition fee, and an annual curriculum fee. The annual curriculum fee is payable at the start of each year. The grade-specific tuition fee is payable monthly over 12 months, per term (January, May and September), or annually in advance. Term-in-advance payment attracts a 5% discount off tuition. Multiple enrolments (e.g. siblings) attract a 5% discount off the tuition of simultaneously enrolled younger siblings.
